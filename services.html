What even is SEO?

SEO, which stands for search engine optimization, is the act of optimizing a website to rank for certain search terms. Our team of SEO experts start by determining the best search terms (keywords) based on your website's overall search relevance by carefully studying a combination of both keyword traffic and keyword competitiveness. Once the target keywords have been selected, the SEO campaign can officially kick off!
